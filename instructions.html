Overview

Rat Race Escape - Game Guide

A hybrid financial education strategy game focused on teaching personal finance, investing, and smart decision-making through engaging simulation.

Start Playing →

How to Play

  1. Create your character and pick a trait (Frugal, Risk Taker, Analyst, Entrepreneur).
  2. Each turn represents one month. Work to earn income, manage expenses, invest, and trade.
  3. Use the Bank to handle deposits, withdrawals, and loans.
  4. Monitor your financial health, mood, and needs from the dashboard.
  5. Respond to random events that can impact your assets, cash flow, and opportunities.
  6. Aim to grow your net worth to $1,000,000+ and keep financial health above 80% to win.

Core Mechanics

  • Turn-based monthly actions (work, invest, trade, learn, bank, spend).
  • Dynamic markets for stocks, bonds, forex, commodities, and crypto.
  • AI competitors, businesses, and corporations influencing prices.
  • Needs & Wants system with mood and physical health impacts.
  • Random events affecting markets, cash, and assets.

Highlights & Features

  • Advanced logging system for transactions, events, and finances.
  • Bank system with credit score, savings, loans, and loan terms.
  • Investment opportunities: businesses, auctions, property deals.
  • Responsive dashboard UI optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • Save & continue feature with browser cache and shareable save keys.

Educational Focus

The game emphasizes:

  • Budgeting and cash flow management.
  • Diversified investing and risk assessment.
  • Credit scoring, loan management, and interest mechanics.
  • Emergency funds, insurance-like random events, and financial resilience.
  • Long-term planning to escape the rat race through assets and passive income.

Getting Started Tips

  • Work early turns to build a cash buffer before investing aggressively.
  • Use the Bank to manage savings and only take loans you can repay.
  • Diversify your portfolio—don’t rely on a single asset class.
  • Keep needs satisfied to avoid mood and health penalties.
  • Monitor financial logs to understand trends in income and expenses.
  • Generate and copy your Save Key regularly to secure progress.

Mobile & Flex Play

The dashboard layout is responsive and adapts to smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Use portrait mode for compact view and landscape for expanded dashboards. Touch-friendly buttons and modals ensure comfortable gameplay on mobile browsers.
